Subtle Asian Networking (SAN)

Subtle Asian Networking is a predominantly Asian community that provides a safe space for young Asian professionals develop themselves professionally while finding solidarity with others who have similar experiences.

Asians in the Americas Support and Professional Network (AASPN)

Asians in the Americas Support and Professional Network (AASPN) is a non for profit professional organization and support group aimed at advancing Asian and Asian-Canadian/American causes on multiple fronts.

Our group was formed out of a mutually recognized need among friends for solutions to issues of media and cultural representation; systematic discrimination in the workplace and elsewhere; in addition to addressing other serious concerns obstructing the Asian community. We hope that by fostering professional relationships and a healthy dialogue around such issues, we can help ameliorate these problems.
